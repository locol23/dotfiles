## Local overlay

`install.sh` syncs the listed ECC subdirs verbatim — files with the same
names as those in ECC will be **overwritten on every sync**. Keep local
customisations in files that ECC does NOT ship, so they survive:

- `common/{agents,code-review,development-workflow,git-workflow,performance}.md` (no ECC equivalent)
- `common/local.md` — cross-language preferences (SOLID, Composition, Testing Trophy, etc.)
- `golang/local.md` — Go-specific local additions
- `typescript/local.md` — TypeScript-specific local additions

**Rule of thumb:** put new house-style content in the corresponding
`<dir>/local.md` rather than editing `coding-style.md` etc.; ECC sync
will not touch `local.md` because it does not exist upstream.
